About my research

From 2012 to 2021, I conducted academic research, both leading and collaborating with other researchers on theory crafting, data collection, data analysis, report writing, and presenting. My research uses statistical techniques derived from the social, natural, and formal sciences, such as social network analysis, allometric modeling, and distribution pitting on survey, experimental research, and archival data.

Broadly, my research focuses on delivering actionable insights into organizational problems such as the following:

  • How to lead virtual teams successfully
  • How to identify, attract, and develop star talent
  • How to maximize power in organizational social networks
