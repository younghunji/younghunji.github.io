About Me

I am passionate about using data analytics to generate actionable solutions and recommendations to complex business problems. Trained in management research using statistical techniques, I have over a decade of experience working with data and communicating data processes and findings through scientific reports, visualizations, and presentations.

I was born in South Korea, moved to New Zealand at a young age, spent my undergraduate and doctoral years in the U.S., and then moved to Finland in 2018. I completed my Ph.D. in Management and Strategy at The George Washington University.
