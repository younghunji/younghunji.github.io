About Me

My passion is using data to generate actionable solutions to business problems. Trained in organizational research using statistical techniques, I have a decade of experience testing hypotheses with quantitative data and storytelling scientific methods and findings through reports, visualizations, and presentations.

I was born in South Korea, moved to New Zealand at a young age, spent my undergraduate and doctoral years in the U.S., and then moved to Finland in 2018. I completed my Ph.D. in Management and Strategy at The George Washington University.
